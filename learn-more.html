Get Involved



Why should I join BPA?

Joining Business Professionals of America (BPA) is a fun way to build leadership qualities, connect with your peers, and explore new interests. You'll have the chance to take on leadership roles, compete in your events, and get involved in your school community. It'll open doors to many opportunities in the future. It's a fantastic way to challenge yourself, have fun, and create lasting memories!

Who can join?

To join the middle level CCSA BPA chapter, you must be a 6th, 7th, or 8th grader who attends Cab Calloway School of the Arts. 7th and 8th graders can compete at regional, state, and national levels, but 6th graders can still join to gain new skills and experience. To join the chapter, ask our advisor, Ms. Mercer, to add you to the Schoology group!

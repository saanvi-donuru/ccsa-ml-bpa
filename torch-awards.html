Torch Awards


The Business Professionals of America Torch Awards program helps people become great leaders and develop important skills. Students can participate in different categories such as leadership, service, cooperation, knowledge, friendship, love, hope, and faith, and citizenship. Each activity is 5, 10, 15, or 20 points. Members should log their activities on the online Torch Resume.

(Business Professionals of America. “Torch Awards - Business Professionals of America.” Business Professionals of America, https://bpa.org/torch-awards/. Accessed 23 December 2024.)

Recognition

Executive Torch Award

This award is given in your local chapter and requires 10 points in each of the 7 categories. The awards given are a pin and a certificate.

Diplomat Torch Award

This award could be given at the local, regional, or state level and requires 30 points in each category. The awards given are a pin and a certificate.

Statesman Torch Award

This award is given at the state level and requires 50 points in each category. The awards given are a pin and certificate.

Ambassador Torch Award

This award is given at the national level and requires 70 points in each category. The awards given are a pin, certificate, and going to the NLC.



(Business Professionals of America. “Torch Awards - Business Professionals of America.” Business Professionals of America, https://bpa.org/torch-awards/. Accessed 23 December 2024.)

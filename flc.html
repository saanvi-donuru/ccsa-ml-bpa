Fall Leadership Conference


Cab Calloway School of the Arts
Middle Level Officers
at the
2024 Fall Leadership Conference

This year, our team had an amazing experience at the Fall Leadership Conference. They became friends with new officers from different schools such as AGW and P.S DuPont. They learned the four pillars of leadership: hope, trust, stability, and compassion. The engaging and fun sessions taught our officers many new skills.





The four pillars of leadership:

Hope

Students explored hope with a fun balloon activity, but there was a twist: every round they could only use either their hands, legs, or head. Students learned that even in difficult situations, they should still be determined.







Trust

In the trust session, one person in the group had to be blindfolded and the other people guided them through an obstacle course with verbal directions. Members reflected on the importance of trust and teamwork.







Stability

Using limited materials, participants worked with new partners to build a phone holder. This activity emphasized on creating strong foundations and the cruciality of teamwork skills that they might need in real-world scenarios.







Compassion

Members wrote things they were grateful for on slips of paper, and then the instructors made a chain, showing students how many things they were lucky to have. The instructors told the students thoughtful ways to show gratitude.
